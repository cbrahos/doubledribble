Double Dribble started as a side project of mine in 2019. I wanted to consolidate all of my art and music ventures under the moniker I previously used during my residency at KCPR. In the time since then, I’ve created cover art for albums that I love and have DJ’d events I would have never dreamed of attending two years ago. Double Dribble is all about having fun with your friends and using art/music to connect with others.
